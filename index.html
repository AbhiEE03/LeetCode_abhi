01 | PART ONE

What is JEE Main?

Objective

Primary gateway to NITs, IIITs, and GFTIs. Also serves as the qualifying exam for JEE Advanced (IITs).

Key Details

  • Conducted by: NTA
  • Mode: CBT (Online)
  • Frequency: Twice a Year (Jan/Apr)
  • Languages: 13 Regional Languages

Papers

  • Paper 1: B.E. / B.Tech
  • Paper 2A: B.Arch
  • Paper 2B: B.Planning

JEE Main at a Glance

1. National Level Exam

Standardized CBT for B.E./B.Tech admissions across India.

2. Twice a Year

Jan & April Sessions. Best of two scores is considered.

3. Gateway to Institutes

Direct admission to 31 NITs (including NIT Mizoram), 26 IIITs, and 33 GFTIs.

Eligibility Criteria (2025 Cycle)

Criteria Details & Requirements
Age Limit No upper age limit. Must meet Class 12 passing year criteria.
Attempts 3 consecutive years starting from the year you first appear for Class 12.
Qualifying Exam Passed Class 12 in 2023, 2024 OR Appearing in 2025.
Institute Admission NITs/IIITs: Min 75% in Class 12 (65% for SC/ST).
OR Top 20 Percentile of Board (MBSE).

The Mizoram Advantage

50% Home State Quota

NIT Mizoram reserves half its seats for students who passed Class 12 in Mizoram.

The Strategy

You are not competing with All-India students. You are only competing with the local pool.

"Even with a low score, the HS quota makes admission viable."
